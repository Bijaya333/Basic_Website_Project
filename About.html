ABOUT MACBOOK COMPANY

Apple Inc., formerly known as Apple Computer, Inc., is an American producer of desktop and laptop computers, mobile phones, tablets, computer peripherals, and software. It was the first prosperous personal computer business and helped make the graphical user interface more common. The company's headquarters are in Cupertino, California.

APPLE PRODUCTS

Apple Inc. (Apple) develops, produces, and sells smartphones, laptops, tablets, wearable technology, accessories, and a variety of related services. iPhone, Mac, iPad, AirPods, Apple TV, Apple Watch, Beats products, HomePod, iPod touch, and accessories are just a few of the company's offerings.

APPLE HEADQUARTERS

Silicon Valley in California includes the city of Cupertino. Although it is most known as the location of Apple's corporate headquarters, Rancho San Antonio Open Space Preserve, a park with a working farm, is also located there. In the Fremont Older Open Space Preserve, there are riding trails. Formerly a horse ranch, the McClellan Ranch Preserve now houses a nature museum.
